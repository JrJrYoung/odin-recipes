Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted room temp butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, room temp
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 24 choccie kiss candies, unwrapped (lol)

Coating

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup red sanding sugar and 1/4 green sanding sugar

Instructions

  1. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Beat butter in a stand mixer until creamy. beat in granulated sugar and brown sugar until blended. Add egg. Add peanut butter and vanilla extract.
  3. Add dry to wet and mix. Cover and chill for 1 hour to 3 days.
  4. Remove dough and preheat oven to 350F. Line 2 large baking sheets.
  5. Roll the now room temp dough into cookie balls that are about 1.5 tbsps each. Roll each ball in sugar until top is coated.
  6. Bake for 11-13 minutes until the top begins to crack, remove cookies and allow to cool 10 mins, press kisses into top.
  7. Eat them all!!! "Om nom nom nom!"
