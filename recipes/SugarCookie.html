How to Make Sugar Cookies with the Cookie Monster

Description

"butt deez ar cookie munsters leest favurite cookies! Haf too wate fur houws! FESFSGHROHKBNKO *cookie rage*"

- The "Cookie Monster", Sep 11, 2009, being told by HR that he cannot fly into a "Cookie Rage" whenever he is upset at work

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups unsalted butter, room temp
  • 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 large room temp egg
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract for taste
  • Royal Icing

Instructions

  1. Whisk the flour, baking powder and salt together.
  2. In a seperate bowl, beat butter and sugar until creamed. Add egg, vanilla and almond extract and beat together.
  3. add dry to wet and mix.
  4. Divide into 2 parts, roll out until 1/4 inch thick on the pan it will be baked on
  5. Chill 1 hour to 2 days after covering with a towel or wrapping with plastic wrap
  6. preheat oven 350F. Cut cookies. Bake for 11-12 minutes until golden brown on edges.
  7. Remove cookies then allow to cool 5 mins, then allow to cool the rest of the way before decorating.
  8. Eat them all!!! "om nom nomn nom!"
